Donation Process

The donation process from the time you arrive center until the time you leave. The blood donation process can be broken down into four steps:

1 - Registration

Request blood within your neighborhood. We match you with a blood donor in your area. Find blood donors near your location...

2 - Seeing

Before you donate, an employee from the blood bank will ask you some confidential questions about your health and lifestyle...

3 - Donation

When it’s time to donate, here’s what will happen: You’ll go into a donor room where you’ll lie down on a cot...

4 - Save Life

You’ll want to sit and relax for at least 10 minutes to restore your strength and get some energy back before you leave...
